Demystifying Your Rights

Learn what the police are allowed to do

And what they're not

This app was created because there seems to be a lack of understanding about the powers of the police.

This quiz has two purposes: information and education.

Education: Teaching people what their rights mean when they get arrested, the app takes you through a series of questions about arrest, stop and search, and search warrants in the UK.

Information: As you go through the quiz, your answer choices are recorded and saved. Once the quiz is complete you can see how you did compared to everyone else. By collecting this data we are able to understand how much the public understand their rights when interacting with the police, and what they are specifically getting wrong. The hope is that this can become the basis for stronger, targeted education tools in the future.
